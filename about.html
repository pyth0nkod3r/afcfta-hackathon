About The Hackathon

Transforming African Trade Through Digital Innovation

The AFCFTA Digital Trade Protocol Hackathon is a groundbreaking initiative aimed at accelerating the implementation of digital solutions for cross-border trade in Africa. This 5-day event brings together the continent's brightest minds to develop innovative solutions that will shape the future of trade across Africa.

With a focus on practical implementation and real-world impact, participants will work on solutions for:

  • Digital Trade Documentation Systems
  • Cross-border Payment Solutions
  • Supply Chain Transparency
  • Regulatory Compliance Platforms
