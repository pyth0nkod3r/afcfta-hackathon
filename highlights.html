Key Features

1. Focus Areas

  • Digital trade facilitation tools
  • Regulatory infrastructure for e-documentation
  • Trust services, authentication, and digital identities
  • Logistics and last-mile delivery optimization

2. Team Structure

  • Total Teams: 10
  • Team Members: 5 (multidisciplinary composition for holistic problem-solving)

3. Mentorship

Experts from trade, technology, and logistics industries will provide ongoing guidance.

4. Participant Recruitment

  • Total Participants: 50
  • Participants selected based on expertise in technology, trade, logistics, and business strategy

5. Follow-Up Support

  • Incubation and funding for winning solutions
  • Opportunities to pilot solutions with regional trade organizations

Impact Goals

  • Drive innovation for AFCFTA implementation
  • Build a tech-enabled ecosystem for seamless trade across Africa
  • Empower participants to contribute to digital trade policy and practice improvements
