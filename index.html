About This Event

The AFCFTA Digital Trade Protocol (DTP) Hackathon, spearheaded by CHERITHCODE CONCEPT LTD, will be held during the Science of Trade Conference 2025, from April 30 to May 3, 2025.
This dynamic event is designed to harness the power of technology to tackle key challenges in implementing the AFCFTA Digital Trade Protocol.

AFCFTA HACKATHON

5-Day Hackathon Plan for AFCFTA Digital Trade Protocol Implementation

The hackathon will serve as a platform to bring together a diverse group of developers, policymakers, startups, and industry experts to co-create actionable innovations, aligning with the broader goals of the AFCFTA and the Science of Trade Conference 2025.
