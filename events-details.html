AFCFTA Digital Trade Protocol Hackathon

The AFCFTA Digital Trade Protocol (DTP) Hackathon, spearheaded by CHERITH-CODE CONCEPT LTD, will be held during the Science of Trade Conference 2025. This dynamic event is designed to harness the power of technology to tackle key challenges in implementing the AFCFTA Digital Trade Protocol.

Participants will collaborate to develop innovative solutions aimed at digitalizing trade processes, enhancing regulatory infrastructure, and improving logistics systems across Africa. The hackathon serves as a platform to bring together developers, policymakers, startups, and industry experts to co-create actionable innovations.

Hackathon Schedule

Day 1: Orientation and Keynote Sessions

Theme: Understanding AFCFTA Digital Trade Protocol and Opportunities

  • Welcome Address and Introduction
  • Keynote Speeches on Digital Trade and Innovation
  • Panel Discussion and Workshops
  • Team Formation and Ideation

Days 2-4: Development and Mentorship

  • Intensive Development Sessions
  • Expert Mentorship and Guidance
  • Progress Reviews and Feedback

Day 5: Final Presentations and Awards

  • Solution Demonstrations to Expert Panel
  • Awards Ceremony and Prize Distribution
  • Networking and Celebration
